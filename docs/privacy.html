Wordiest Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-16

Summary

Wordiest does not run analytics, does not show ads, and does not collect personal data. Your game history and rating are stored locally on your device.

Data stored on device

  • Your local game history, rating, and settings.

Game Center

If you sign in to Game Center, Apple may process information needed for leaderboards and achievements. Wordiest uses Game Center APIs, but does not receive your Apple ID.
